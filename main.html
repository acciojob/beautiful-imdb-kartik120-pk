The Conjuring: Last Rites is scheduled for release in theaters on September 5, 2025. It will be the fourth and final film in The Conjuring franchise. Plot: The Conjuring: Last Rites is set in the early 1980s and follows Ed and Lorraine Warren as they investigate a series of unexplained deaths in a Spanish monastery. The Warrens uncover a sinister force linked to an ancient religious relic
